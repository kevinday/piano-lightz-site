piano.lightz

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

piano.lightz is used to create and publish piano visualizer videos for the piano.lightz TikTok account. This workflow may process limited account and publishing data needed to connect to TikTok and publish content.

Information processed

  • TikTok account identifiers returned during authorization, such as display name, username, and open ID.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required to maintain an authorized TikTok connection.
  • Video files, captions, hashtags, and publishing settings selected for TikTok posts.
  • Basic post status information returned by TikTok after upload or publication attempts.

How information is used

  • To authenticate the piano.lightz TikTok account.
  • To publish authorized video content to TikTok.
  • To display upload and publication status in the workflow.

Sharing

Information is shared only with TikTok as required to authorize the account and publish selected content.

Retention

Authorization data and publishing metadata may be retained locally for operational continuity until removed or rotated.

Contact

For privacy questions related to piano.lightz, use the contact method associated with the GitHub repository or site owner.
