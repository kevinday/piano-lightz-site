piano.lightz

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

piano.lightz is used to create and publish piano visualizer videos. This workflow may process limited account and publishing data needed to connect to external publishing services and deliver content.

Information processed

  • Account identifiers returned during authorization, such as display name, username, and platform IDs.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required to maintain an authorized publishing connection.
  • Video files, captions, hashtags, and publishing settings selected for outbound posts.
  • Basic post status information returned after upload or publication attempts.

How information is used

  • To authenticate connected publishing accounts.
  • To publish authorized video content to external platforms.
  • To display upload and publication status in the workflow.

Sharing

Information is shared only with connected third-party services as required to authorize an account and publish selected content.

Retention

Authorization data and publishing metadata may be retained locally for operational continuity until removed or rotated.

Contact

For privacy questions related to piano.lightz, use the contact method associated with the GitHub repository or site owner.
