piano.lightz

Short-form piano visualizers for TikTok.

piano.lightz is the production workflow behind the piano.lightz TikTok account. It is used to source MIDI files, generate vertical piano visualizer videos, prepare captions and post settings, and publish finished clips to the connected TikTok account.

What this workflow does

  • Imports piano-focused MIDI sources for evaluation and rendering.
  • Generates styled vertical piano-roll videos with audio and title overlays.
  • Prepares final caption text, hashtags, privacy settings, and posting metadata.
  • Publishes finished videos to the piano.lightz TikTok account.
